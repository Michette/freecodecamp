The Hollowfication Incident

How did it happen?

The Incident
  • Kisuke Urahara promoted to captain of the 12th Division.
  • Urahara creates the Shinigami Research and Development Institute.
  • Urahara reveals the Nest of Maggots to Hiyori Sarugaki.
  • Urahara recruits Mayuri Kurotsuchi as his 3rd Seat & Vice-President of the S.R.D.I.
  • Captain Kensei Muguruma saves Shūhei Hisagi and other children from a giant Hollow.
  • The Elite of the 9th Division disappear.
  • A task force is sent to investigate the 9th Division disappearance & engaged hollowfied 9th division members in battle.
  • The task force is attacked and undergoes forced Hollowfication.
  • Lieutenant Sōsuke Aizen alongside 3rd Seat Gin Ichimaru & 5th Seat Kaname Tōsen is reveled to be behind everything.
  • Urahara & Grand Kidō Chief Tessai Tsukabishi show up to save the task force.
  • Urahara attempts to reverse the hollowfication using the Hōgyoku but "fails".
  • Urahara and Tessai are framed and tried, but escape alongside the hollowfied Shinigami with Captain Yoruichi Shihōin's help.
