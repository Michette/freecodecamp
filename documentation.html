One

While passing through the Dangai Ichigo and his friends are chased by the Kōtotsu. This resulted in a gain of eight days due to a time anomaly.

Two

Arrival in Soul Society.

  • Ichigo Kurosaki vs. Jidanbō Ikkanzaka
  • Orihime heals Jidanbō
  • Ichigo meets Ganju Shiba
    • Three

    Ichigo and friends meet Kūkaku Shiba and spend the night at her house while she prepares the cannon and Ichigo works on controlling his spirit energy to create a barrier.

    Four

    Kūkaku Shiba shoots Ichigo and companions into Seireitei. The group gets separated.

    • Ichigo Kurosaki vs. Ikkaku Madarame
    • Ganju Shiba vs. Yumichika Ayasegawa
    • Uryū Ishida vs. Jirōbō Ikkanzaka
    Five
    • Ichigo Kurosaki vs. Renji Abarai:Rematch

    Byakuya orders Renji thrown in jail Hanataro spends the night healing Ichigo and discovers that a Hollow mask has appeared on Ichigo's body to protect him from Renji's blow.

    Six

    Conspiracy's Masterstroke: Illusory Assassination.

    • Ichigo Kurosaki vs. Kenpachi Zaraki
    • Yasutora Sado vs. Shunsui Kyōraku
    Seven

    First day of the Bankai Training.

    • Uryū Ishida vs. Mayuri Kurotsuchi
    • Tōshirō Hitsugaya vs. Gin Ichimaru
    Eight

    Zaraki decides to help Ichigo.

    • Kenpachi Zaraki vs. Kaname Tōsen & Sajin Komamura
    • Ikkaku Madarame vs. Tetsuzaemon Iba
    • Yumichika Ayasegawa vs. Shūhei Hisagi
    Nine
    • Renji Abarai vs. Byakuya Kuchiki

    Second day of the Bankai Training.

    Ten

    Rukia's Execution begins but Ichigo interrupts.

    • Ichigo Kurosaki vs. Byakuya Kuchiki: Rematch
    • Genryūsai Shigekuni Yamamoto vs. Shunsui Kyōraku & Jūshirō Ukitake
    • Yoruichi Shihōin vs. Suì-Fēng

    Isshin Kurosaki regains Shinigami powers.

    Eleven

    Hitsugaya finds Central 46 has been massacred.

    • Rangiku Matsumoto vs. Izuru Kira

    Aizen is revealed to be alive. Aizen stabs Hinamori and critically injures Hitsugaya

    Twelve

    Aizen reveals his influence on all events. Aizen escape to Hueco Mundo with Gin and Tōsen. Ichigo and his friends are credited with saving Soul Society.

    Thirteen

    Ichigo and his friends spend one week in Soul Society. Rukia informs Ichigo that she is remaining in Soul Society. Ukitake presents Ichigo with a Substitute Shinigami Badge. Group returns home, where Urahara meets them and apologizes to Ichigo.
