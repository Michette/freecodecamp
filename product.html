How does it work?

You will have two(2) free changes during the process, so make sure that you are satisfied as every change after that will be charged.

There is a complexity rate of 5% to 50%, depending of how complex the project is for me and it will be discussed beforehand.

You can add more characters for 5-15€ each, depending on the complexity and the option that you choose. Feel free to send me a message to talk about your ideas!

Payments will be made before starting through Paypal (there might be some Paypal fee that you'll need to take into consideration).

Things I do:

  • Humans, humanoids
  • Customs fanart, ships and group
  • Light blood, injuries
  • Common weapons, imaginary weapons

Things I don't do:

  • NSFW
  • Complex backgrounds
  • Furry, mecha/robots
  • Gore, heavy armors
